WHO WE ARE

Your Premier Indigenous EPC Partner

Established in 2001, PWO Offshore is a full-service EPC company specializing in the design and fabrication of offshore living quarters, topsides, and helidecks for both Nigerian and international energy clients, with deep expertise in the Gulf of Guinea's unique requirements.
  • Design and Fabrication of Life camps and Lifeboats.
  • Design and Fabrication of Modular Living Quarters.
  • Project Management.
